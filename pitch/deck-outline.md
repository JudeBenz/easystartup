## Slide 1 · Hook
**"What happens when your best operator retires?"**
- Their process leaves with them; every new hire relearns from scratch
- Subtitle: Business operations OS for the physical world

## Slide 2 · Problem
**"Knowledge lives in heads, not in systems."**
Three pain columns:
1. Tribal knowledge — SOPs in binders nobody reads
2. Compliance gaps — certs expire, OSHA risk accumulates
3. Training bottleneck — weeks of shadowing, not repeatable

## Slide 3 · Platform
**"One platform. Three stages. From binder to robot."**
- Stage 1 · Procedurize (Live) — AI editor, player, quiz, cert
- Stage 2 · Autopilot (Live) — morning run, blocked beats, twin
- Stage 3 · Robot-ready (Roadmap) — auto-translated execution spec
